About the Platform

This tool helps municipal water authorities, rural developers, and engineers assess the suitability of a site for well drilling. Using AI and NAQUIM datasets, our system predicts aquifer depth, potential water yield, recommended drilling techniques, and water quality forecasts — tailored to your chosen location.
